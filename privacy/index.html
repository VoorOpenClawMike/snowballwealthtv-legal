Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

1. What We Collect

We only store the TikTok OAuth access token required to post videos on your behalf. This token is stored securely on your local machine and is not transmitted to any external server.

2. What We Do Not Collect

We do not collect personal information, analytics, behavioral data, location data, or any other user data beyond what is strictly necessary to post videos.

3. Third Parties

We never sell, share, or transmit your data to third parties. The only external service used is TikTok's official API.

4. Data Retention

Your OAuth token is stored locally. You can delete it at any time, and revoke access via TikTok Settings → Security → Authorized Apps.
